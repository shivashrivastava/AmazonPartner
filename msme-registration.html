Types of MSMEs


Micro Enterprise

Get Exempt from the Income Tax:- You can enjoy the tax benefits in your initial years of business and can save lakhs of the amount by registering with MSME.

Small Enterprise

A Small Enterprises utilizes a few numbers of laborers and have low sales. The small company can have more than 10 workers and less than 50 workers. The Small Enterprise may include hotels, dry cleaners, etc. The funds invested in the Small Enterprise can be more than Rs 25 Lakhs and less than Rs 5 Crores.

Medium Enterprise

Medium Enterprise companies have less than 250 workers and the investments done in the Medium Enterprise must be between Rs 5 Crores to Rs 10 Crores.
